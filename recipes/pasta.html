Bacon and Pumpkin Pesto Pasta

Description

15 minutes prep, 25 minutes cook and 4 servings

You can make the pesto for this hearty dish from scratch, or you could take a shortcut with a ready-made version. Either way, this pasta is a mid-week family pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1kg kent pumpkin, deseeded, peeled, cut into 2.5cm pieces
  • Olive oil spray
  • Salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • 400g penne rigate
  • 300g short-cut bacon, thinly sliced
  • 125g (1/2 cup) basil pesto
  • Shaved parmesan, to serve
  • salt, to season

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking tray with non-sticking baking paper. Place the pumpkin, in a single layer, on the prepared tray. Spray with olive oil spray and season with salt and pepper. Bake in oven for 25 minutes or until tender.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in a large saucepan of salted boiling water following packet directions or until al dente. Drain and return to the pan.
  3. Cook the bacon in a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat, stirring for 5 minutes or until crisp and golden.
  4. Add the pesto to the pasta and toss until coated. Add the pumpkin and bason and toss until well combined. Divide among serving bowls and sprinkle with parmesan. Serve immediately.
